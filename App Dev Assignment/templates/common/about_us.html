Singtopia

Founded in mid 2021, we the founders, Aik Leong, Jia Jun, aaralynn and kai kai were inspired by the fast growing digital economy in the current period along with the pandemic to create something to help companies who are suffering without digitization. Thus Singtopia was born. A website to target various SMEs to conduct an online business to catch up with digitization and help bring potential to the various SMEs to become a big business and provide many different kinds of products for our well valued customers which is you. In the process, our business has gain many partnerships and are planning to set up headquarters in other countries to expand the business.
